To My Sister in Christ

From Serena  ·  With Love & Gratitude
🎙 Reading aloud to you… Ready
Dear Sister,

I am writing this to you today because I need you to know how deeply I see God's hand in our connection. I do not believe in coincidences, and I certainly do not believe that our paths crossed by accident. God put you in my life right when I needed you the most.

When I reached out and invited you in, I was — and still am — going through some of the hardest moments with my health and dealing with severe injuries. It is a heavy season. But I believed in what God placed before us both, and I know in my heart that together we can build something truly big. I have been learning, growing, and waiting for the right time, trusting that He placed this on us for a reason.

I may not know you personally yet, but I know you spiritually. I feel it in my spirit that God orchestrated this for us both. It was set in stone for us before we even thought about being in each other's lives. The way you send Bible verses daily, like clockwork, is a constant reminder to me that He is at the center of everything we are building together.

"I thank my God every time I remember you. In all my prayers for all of you, I always pray with joy because of your partnership in the gospel from the first day until now, being confident of this, that He who began a good work in you will carry it on to completion until the day of Christ Jesus." — Philippians 1:3–6

That third strand is Him. He brought us together not just for business, but to uplift each other, to support each other, and to walk this season together in faith. You have been a light to me when things were heavy, and I am so grateful for your presence, your daily encouragement, and your spirit.

I love the Lord, and I love you. I am so excited for what we are building together and for the blessings God has already set in store for both of us. This is rare. What we have is rare. And I do not take it lightly.

With all my love and gratitude,
LovelyLady from Serena

P.S. — From my heart to yours…

In what I am building for us, and for you to step into, I have huge faith in you — without even meeting you personally. I have faith in you. I know we can do this. I know I can show you how, and we will win big time. Because this is what God gave us. Oh yeah. Let's do it, Sister.

I am waiting on a few things to line up, but as soon as they do, I am going to holler at you and we will start. From the very first day. Because we are going to hit the ground running — texts, calls, whatever it takes. I do not know every detail of how it will look, but I know it is supposed to be this. I know He put it in my lap for a reason, and He made me sit down and learn everything I have learned.

So hopefully I can learn and then teach you everything you need to know on your end. That way we can just go, girl. And I mean go. Let's do this.

A gift of music — just for you 🕊️
