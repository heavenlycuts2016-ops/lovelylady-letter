To My Sister in Christ

Dear Sister,

I am writing this to you today because I need you to know how deeply I see God's hand in our connection. I don't believe in coincidences, and I certainly don't believe that our paths crossed by accident. God put you in my life right when I needed you the most.

When you first called and invited me into what you were selling, I was going through some of the worst moments with my health and dealing with severe injuries. It was a dark and difficult season. But in that exact moment, you reached out. And in return, I welcomed you into a chance to do sales together—an opportunity that I truly believe will change both of our lives.

I may not know you personally yet, but I know you spiritually. I feel it in my spirit that God orchestrated this for us both. It was set in stone for us before we even thought about being in each other's lives. The way we share Bible verses daily, like clockwork, is a constant reminder to me that He is at the center of this partnership.

"Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor: If either of them falls down, one can help the other up... A cord of three strands is not quickly broken." — Ecclesiastes 4:9-10, 12

That third strand is Him. He brought us together not just for business, but to uplift each other, to support each other, and to walk this season together in faith. You have been a light to me when things were heavy, and I am so grateful for your presence, your daily encouragement, and your spirit.

I love the Lord, and I love you. I am so excited for what we are building and for the blessings God has in store for both of us.

With all my love and gratitude,
LovelyLady from Serena
